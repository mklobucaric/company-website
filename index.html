Examples of AI Application Usage

  • Content Generation: Creating personalized marketing messages, promotional content, and automated summary generation with advanced text analysis.
  • Content Analysis: Multimodal analysis and insight generation from texts, images, and other sources. Deep understanding of textual content optimizes data management.
  • Automated Responses: Generating automatic replies to emails, creating orders via chatbot, and similar tasks.

  • Content Classification: Includes texts, images, audio recordings, sentiment analysis, spam detection, and object recognition.
  • Categorization: Grouping content by semantic similarity and context. Categorizing documents such as invoices and bills by supplier, expense category, or project.
  • Labeling: Recognizing and labeling entities (e.g., names, locations, or business information), keywords, topics, sentiments, and categorizing content in text or images.

  • Semantic Search: Advanced search of unstructured data by meaning (or hybrid, by meaning and keywords) within private documents/data (text, images), retrieving relevant segments and generating summaries with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology.
  • Data Extraction: Extracting information from text and images (invoices, tables) and their categorization. Includes delivering information in the desired format or automatically populating spreadsheets or databases with extracted data.
  • Multimodal Search: Advanced multimodal search of unstructured data involving text and images. Suitable for precise identification and categorization of textual and visual content or searching based on image queries. In more advanced implementations, it may include audio and video.

  • Administrative Actions: Using AI technologies to automate routine or more complex administrative tasks through a series of connected actions (e.g., defined in a flowchart) as in the above examples.
  • Business Processes with Agents and Tools: Using AI agents in the role of various personas/experts with the ability to analyze, reason, and make autonomous decisions about the order and type of actions in real time, extending functionality by using different external tools for actions such as: internet searching, calculators, retrieving weather forecasts, using Google Maps for route optimization, etc., and generating documents in the desired format.
  • Data Management: Interpreting user queries and converting them into SQL queries for operational needs.

  • Customer Support Agents: Development of specialized interactive chatbot agents for automatically responding to inquiries, providing customer support, and performing specific tasks. Chatbot agents can integrate with existing systems and be customized for natural conversation and effective interaction with users.
  • Internal Knowledge Bases with RAG: Building a dynamic knowledge base using RAG technology for instant access to instructions, guidelines, and technical documentation. Includes an AI assistant for conversational interaction.
  • Orchestration of AI Agents: Coordinating different AI agents to perform tasks such as web research, downloading and analyzing content, creating offers, and quality checking. AI agents, each expert in their own field, collaborate to deliver high-quality results for the assigned tasks.
